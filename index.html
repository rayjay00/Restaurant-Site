Our Story

Established in 2011, Burrito Loco was founded by our family from Michoacán, Mexico. Built in the bustling city of Fayetteville, Arkansas, all the recipes are created by us. We like to experiment with new salsas every week and all of the chicken and pork tamales are made by hand. For those nights (or gamedays) you feel like having a drink, we have a stocked bar filled with top shelf alcohol, fresh margaritas, and local beer from Ozark and Fossil Cove. Come to Burrito Loco for the best Mexican food in Fayetteville, Arkansas!
